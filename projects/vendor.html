Vendor Analytics

Vendor Performance Suite

Supplier scorecard and shortage analysis for strategic vendor management

Focus Area Vendor Management
Technology Power BI, SQL
KPI Types Fill Rate, Shorts

The Challenge

Managing vendor performance requires tracking multiple KPIs across hundreds of suppliers. Procurement teams needed a unified view of fill rates, in-stock percentages, and shortage values to drive vendor negotiations and identify at-risk suppliers.

Key Business Questions

  • Which vendors have the worst fill rate performance?
  • What is the total dollar exposure from vendor shorts?
  • How do DC out-of-stock rates trend over fiscal weeks?
  • Which product categories have the highest shortage risk?

The Solution

I built a two-dashboard vendor performance suite:

📊 Vendor Scorecard

Comprehensive vendor performance tracking with CSL (Customer Service Level), in-stock percentage trends, and vendor summary comparisons.

📉 Shorts Analysis

Dollar exposure from shortage events with trending analysis showing improvement/degradation patterns over fiscal periods.

🏭 DC OOS Tracking

Distribution center out-of-stock rates correlated with vendor shipment performance.

🎯 Action Items

Prioritized vendor improvement recommendations based on impact and improvement potential.
