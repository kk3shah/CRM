Cart Abandonment from Product Page Friction

Many customers researching supplements have specific questions (e.g., ingredients, dosage, contraindications). If these aren't immediately clear or trust signals are missing on your product pages, they'll often abandon before adding to cart, especially for products with higher perceived risk.

Under-optimized Post-Purchase Upsell/Cross-sell

For a supplements business, there are natural product pairings (e.g., protein powder + creatine, vitamin D + K2). Without a strategically implemented post-purchase offer flow, you're missing out on immediate, high-margin additions to average order value (AOV) from buyers already committed.

Missed Opportunity for Repeat Purchases

Supplements are inherently recurring purchases. If your email flows aren't expertly triggering repeat purchases based on product consumption cycles, or if subscription options aren't compellingly presented and managed, you're leaving significant customer lifetime value (LTV) on the table.
