The Challenge

Culture is Critical, Yet Invisible

Most organizations measure everything except what matters most: how people treat each other

The Problem

  • Burnout and disengagement go undetected until it's too late
  • Emotional labor is invisible and unrewarded
  • Culture assessments are annual, subjective, and actionless
  • Recognition systems favor loudness over impact
  • Toxicity spreads silently through teams

The Kindworth Solution

  • Real-time culture analytics from everyday interactions
  • Kindness Quotient (KQ) scores for individuals and teams
  • Early warning system for burnout and disengagement
  • Recognition tied to measurable positive behaviors
  • Network analysis to identify culture carriers
